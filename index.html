Oh, and what I do.

The work bit

If you've scrolled all the way here — hello! I'm Vidhi Agrawal, and I promise this tiny section won't take long.

Professionally, I work as a Digital Journey Specialist — which means I think deeply about how people move through digital experiences, and how to make those journeys feel more intuitive and human.

But honestly? That's just the day job. Everything above this section is the real me. The one who gets genuinely excited about a good bookshop, a mountain road, or the perfect frame.

If you want to talk about any of the above — the travels, the books, the films, the writing — I'm easy to reach.
